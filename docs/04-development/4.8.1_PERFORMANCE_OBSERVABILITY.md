# 4.8.1 — Performance Observability

## Status

Implementation in validation.

## Scope

This slice adds deterministic monitoring for renderer resource pressure and browser long tasks before further cache and asset optimisation.

## Delivered

- draw-call warning at 120 and critical review at 180;
- visible-triangle warning at 750,000 and critical review at 1,200,000;
- active material-program warning at 32;
- bounded texture and geometry pressure checks;
- long-task tracking at the approved 50 ms threshold;
- edge-triggered warning, severity-escalation and recovery events;
- bounded rolling long-task duration snapshots;
- unit coverage for warning, critical, recovery, resource independence and sample eviction.

4. Add sustained iPad interaction and memory-residency validation.
